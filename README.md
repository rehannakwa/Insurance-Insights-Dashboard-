## üìò DAX Summary Table

The following table summarizes the key DAX measures that power the dashboard visuals and insights.

| **Measure Name**          | **Logic (Simplified)**                         | **Purpose**                                  |
|---------------------------|------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------|
| Total Revenue             | Sum of premium amount                          | Main business revenue KPI                    |
| Total Customers           | Unique customer count                          | Customer base size                           |
| Daily Revenue             | Revenue filtered by selected date              | Trend analysis                               |
| Revenue (Previous Day)    | Revenue one day before                         | Growth comparison                            |
| Daily Revenue Growth %    | % change between today and previous day        | Business performance tracking                |
| Revenue by Sales Mode     | Revenue grouped by sales channel               | Channel-level performance                    |
| Customers by Sales Mode   | Customer count per sales channel               | Acquisition source insights                  |
| Revenue by Age Group      | Revenue grouped by age                         | Demographic contribution analysis            |
| Customers by Age Group    | Customer count grouped by age                  | Segmentation insights                        |
| Policies by Age Group     | Policies purchased per age group               | Product adoption patterns                    |
| Settlement % by Age       | Average settlement rate                        | Claims & risk analysis                       |
| Monthly Revenue           | Revenue aggregated by month                    | Seasonality and trend detection              |
| Monthly Customers         | Customer count aggregated by month             | Demand cycle visualization                   |

This simplified table provides a clear picture of the analytical logic used in the dashboard without listing full DAX formulas.
